About Bookable

Bookable is a simple and elegant web platform designed for book lovers who want to discover new reads across various genres. Whether you're looking for fiction, fantasy, poetry, or mental health resources, Bookable makes it easy to explore books through clean design and fast results.

Powered by the Open Library API, Bookable lets you search for books by title, author, or topic—and even dive into curated subjects with a single click. Each book comes with essential details, a cover preview, and a quick link to buy the book on Amazon.

It's minimal. It's fast. It's for readers. Whether you're studying, exploring, or just daydreaming your next read—Bookable helps you find books that matter to you.
